    • About Debug

    Debug is a free, innovative tool to help farmers maximize their yields and make their pesticide uses more effective.

    Farmers rely heavily on crop yields as their main source of income. However, many pests can get in the way of optimal production yields. Pesticides are the best choice for eliminating pests, but if used inefficiently, there are negative consequences such as:

    • The spraying of beneficial insects: Spraying pesticides on beneficial insects can negatively imoact the production rate of a farmer's crops, which reduces profits.
    • Pesticide resistance: The overuse of pesticides can lead insects becoming resistant to pesticides (less likely to be harmed by pesticides), which will result in farmers needing to spend more money on newer, more expensive formulas.
    • Loss to the environment: Excess pesticides can be carried away by the wind, hurting ecosystems and reducing the net amount of the pesticide that helps protect crops.

    Unfortunately, these effects worsen each year, causing rural/small farmers to lose thousands of dollars, as on average, they spend around $21,000 on pesticides alone as of 2019.

    Debug strives to solve all of these issues by heloing farmers identify insects in their fields, classify them as beneficial or harmful, and provide customized recommendations for pesticides depending harmful pests present.
